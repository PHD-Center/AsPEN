The AsPEN Special Interest Group held its annual meeting during the International Society for Pharmacoepidemiology (ISPE)'s 41st Annual Meeting in Washington, DC, on August 25, 2025. The meeting brought together a diverse group of researchers, trainees, and collaborators from across the Asia-Pacific region and beyond.

The session provided an opportunity for both long-standing and new members to introduce themselves, share their research interests, and learn about ongoing activities within the network. With strong participation from multiple countries and regions, the meeting highlighted the growing reach and collaborative spirit of AsPEN.

Members discussed ongoing initiatives, including the development of an AsPEN webinar series and collaborative research projects focused on healthcare databases in the Asia-Pacific region. These discussions generated valuable ideas for future multinational studies and opportunities for collaboration.

In addition to the annual meeting, AsPEN organized a Spotlight Poster Session during ICPE 2025, showcasing research conducted by network members and collaborators. The session provided an opportunity for attendees to learn about ongoing pharmacoepidemiologic research across the Asia-Pacific region.

The meeting further reinforced AsPEN's mission of fostering international collaboration and creating opportunities for researchers to address important medication safety and effectiveness questions through multinational research.
