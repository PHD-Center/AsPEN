title: "PSSA Study 1 — Antipsychotics and diabetes"
year: 2013
order: 1
---

A Prescription Sequence Symmetry Analysis (PSSA) across AsPEN sites examining the association between antipsychotic use and incident diabetes / acute hyperglycaemia.

**Method:** Multi-country rapid adverse drug event assessment using a common protocol applied locally at each AsPEN site.

**Publication:** Pratt N, et al. *Pharmacoepidemiol Drug Saf.* 2013. PMID 23696036.
