A Prescription Sequence Symmetry Analysis (PSSA) across AsPEN sites examining the association between antipsychotic use and incident diabetes / acute hyperglycaemia.

**Method:** Each site executed an identical PSSA protocol locally against its own claims/EHR database; site-level effect estimates were then meta-analysed centrally. This is the canonical AsPEN "common protocol, local execution" pattern.

**Why it mattered:** It demonstrated that signal direction for a metabolic adverse drug event was consistent across heterogeneous Asia-Pacific databases — establishing the methodological credibility of the network and seeding most of AsPEN's subsequent PSSA work.

**Lead publication:** Pratt N, et al. *Pharmacoepidemiol Drug Saf.* 2013. PMID 23696036.
