A multi-country PSSA examining the cardiovascular risk associated with thiazolidinedione use across AsPEN sites.

**Aim:** Compare signal direction and magnitude across heterogeneous Asian healthcare databases using a harmonised PSSA protocol.

**Why it mattered:** Extended the AsPEN signal-detection model from psychiatric drugs (Study 1) into cardiometabolic safety, helping refine the network's "rapid signal triage" workflow.
