---
title: "SCAN — Surveillance of Health Care in Asian Network"
year: 2014
order: 3
---

The SCAN project develops infrastructure for two parallel tracks of postmarketing surveillance:

1. **Signal detection** using sequence-symmetry and disproportionality methods across multiple databases.
2. **Pharmacoepidemiological studies** that follow up promising signals with formal cohort or case-control analyses under a common protocol.
