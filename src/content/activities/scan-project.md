The SCAN project develops infrastructure for two parallel tracks of postmarketing surveillance:

1. **Signal detection** — sequence-symmetry and disproportionality methods run across multiple databases to triage potential drug safety issues quickly.
2. **Pharmacoepidemiological studies** — promising signals are followed up with formal cohort or case-control analyses under a common protocol harmonised across sites.

**Why it matters:** SCAN is the operational scaffolding behind much of AsPEN's later signal-driven work — the "two-track" design (fast triage → confirmatory study) is now the network's default playbook.
